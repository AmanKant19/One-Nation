Our Mission

Knowledge Sharing

We Believed that "Gaining knowledge, is the first step to Wisdom. Sharing it, is the first step to Humanity".

.

Giving Answers

Our main moto was to plant 2 questions in students mind before doing any work, that is "How" and "Why"

.

Career Guidance

We made all the students to believe that any career they choose should have a purpose and we also motivated the patriotism in students by saying the sacrifices that have been done for our country.

Hope

Mainly sharing our knowledge with this students has made us to have a new Hope on future of our nation and we had amazing experience waiting to get more.

Responsibility

This is our responsibility to share our knowledge and to motivate the futures of our Nation because sharing knowledge along with motivation is the best way to educate the students.

Values

Teamwork

Together we can achieve it

With all zeal and confidence the group takes the Inspiration from all the freedom fighters,the farmers and the soldiers of this country who laid down their lives for the greater good of this country.The group believes in universal fraternity and need of the youth to raise and write their own destiny.The group efficiently leveraging the power of social media to disseminate information about their work and reach more and more people.

Help Everyone

Help the Nation by helping others

The group shows a lot of concern in social welfare aspects such as upliftment of poor, education of children, protection of animals, wildlife, environment, improving the status of women etc.The group pursues activities to relieve suffering, promote the interests of the poor, protect the environment, provide basic social services, or undertake community development.With the concept of sharing knowledge and it's patriotic feelings the group visits the schools and colleges and explores the revolutionary ideas of the fellow young fellows.
